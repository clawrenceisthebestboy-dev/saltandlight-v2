Why We Built This

Wellness shouldn't
require a trip to the city.

Salt & Light Wellness was founded on a simple idea: the most powerful healing therapies in the world shouldn't only exist in expensive urban spas or clinical settings. The White Mountains region deserved better.

We brought Medical Grade 2 red light therapy and pharmaceutical-grade halotherapy together in a single private booth — the Red Light / Halotherapy Booth experience — and paired it with PEMI Mat electromagnetic technology. The result is a wellness studio built around real science, real equipment, and real results.

"We're not selling relaxation. We're selling cellular restoration. The peace is a side effect."

Located inside Meraki Salon & Spa on White Mountain Highway in North Conway, NH, we've created a space where members from Conway to Jackson, Bartlett to Tamworth, come for consistent, measurable improvement in how they feel, how they look, and how they recover.
