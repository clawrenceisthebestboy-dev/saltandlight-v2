Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Who We Are

Salt & Light Wellness LLC is a wellness studio located at 2967 White Mountain Highway, inside Meraki Salon & Spa, North Conway, NH. We offer red light therapy, halotherapy, and PEMI Mat sessions. You can reach us at (207) 319-4141 or saltandlightwellnessnh@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

When you contact us or book a session, we may collect:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Health intake information you provide before your first session (stored securely, used only for your care)
  • Messages or questions submitted through our contact form

We do not collect payment information through this website. All payments are handled in person at the studio.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule your sessions
  • Send appointment reminders (if you opt in)
  • Provide appropriate care based on your health intake
  • Improve our services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

Health Information

Any health-related information you share with us — including intake forms and waiver details — is treated with the utmost confidentiality. It is used only to ensure your sessions are safe and effective, and is never shared outside of Salt & Light Wellness without your explicit consent.

Contact Form

When you submit our contact form, your message is delivered to us via Formspree, a secure third-party form service. Formspree's privacy policy can be found at formspree.io. We do not use your submitted information for any purpose other than responding to you.

Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or analytics tools that collect personal data. We do not track your behavior across other websites.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (such as Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you have concerns about sensitive health information, please contact us directly by phone or in person.

Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete any personal information we hold about you at any time by contacting us at saltandlightwellnessnh@gmail.com or calling (207) 319-4141.

Children's Privacy

Our services and website are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us
