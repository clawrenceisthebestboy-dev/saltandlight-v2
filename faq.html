Before Your Visit

Preparing for
your session.

Hydration is the most important thing — drink water before you come in. Your cells heal significantly better when you are well hydrated, and you will feel the difference in your results.

Dress comfortably, and think about what areas of your body you want to target. Red light does not penetrate through clothing, so you will want to expose the skin in those areas during your booth session. That is exactly why our booths are fully wrapped for privacy — so you can get the most out of your therapy without any discomfort.

Just yourself. Wear comfortable clothing. For Red Light / Halotherapy Booth, you may want to bring a hair tie if you prefer to keep your hair up. No special preparation needed. We provide special protective glasses for your eyes — just put them on and relax.

Avoid heavy lotions or oils on the skin before a red light session — they can block light penetration. Otherwise, just show up hydrated and ready to relax.

PEMI Mat

Electromagnetic
therapy.

Every PEMI session begins with 10 minutes of Tranquility Mode — a gentle frequency designed to bring your body into a parasympathetic (rest and heal) state, priming your cells for deeper healing. After that, the remaining 10–20 minutes can be used to target specific areas that need work using different therapeutic frequencies. You recline on a super comfortable chaise lounge chair and feel very little physically — perhaps a faint tingling for some people. The therapeutic effect is happening at the cellular level. Most people find it so profoundly relaxing that many actually fall asleep. The energized, restored feeling comes in the hours after your session — and your body continues to heal for 8–12 hours after you leave.

The PEMI mat is in a shared wellness space — another client may occasionally be using a booth nearby. It's a calm, quiet environment. If you want a fully private session, the Red Light / Halotherapy Booths are completely enclosed.
